My Second Home


My Second Home:


The city of Dublin,


The tescos with all sorts of candies


My favorite place in the whole world: Ireland


From valleys and cliffs,


to a shoreline full of rocks


down to pastures and Irish towns this is the place I love.


Whether it be the beaches with no one there,


or the scenic drive in the middle of nowhere.


It’s like a second home if only I could remember all of it.


When I was young and now looking back,


there are so many memories lost.


If only I could remember my second home.
